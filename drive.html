Google Drive Music Player
Guide

Enter the ID for the Google Drive folder you would like to use and click the 'Login' button. The ID can be found by taking that last part of the folder's URL, for example, drive.google.com/drive/folders/1_gbbbbb-aaaaa has an ID of "1_gbbbbb-aaaaa".
Alternatively,"root" can be used to browse from your top-level folder.
Note: Trust the website and click on proceed in order to login.
